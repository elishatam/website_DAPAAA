Aeriel Ashlee, M.Ed
OPAL Advisor

Aeriel serves as the Assistant Dean and Advisor to Pan Asian students at Dartmouth. Offering one-on-one counseling and advising for individual students, consultation and organizational support for Dartmouth's 20+ Pan Asian-related student groups, and working collaboratively with campus colleagues to advance college-wide equity and inclusion, Aeriel joined the Dartmouth community in August 2012.

WELCOME TO DAPAAA

The Dartmouth Asian Pacific American Alumni Association (DAPAAA) was established in 1998 to foster community among the Asian Pacific American alumni of the College. Through various social programs all over the nation, including networking events, career panels, and philanthropy, DAPAAA aims to further the intellectual, social, cultural and educational well-being of the Asian Pacific American alumni and students of the College, as well as to provide the College and the greater College community, with the active support of the Asian Pacific American alumni in matters of mutual concern.

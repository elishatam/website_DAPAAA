FAQs

Welcome to the Dartmouth Asian Pacific American Alumni Association's Website

WHAT IS DAPAAA?
DAPAAA stands for Dartmouth Asian Pacific American Alumni Association. DAPAAA is one of many networks open to Dartmouth alumni. We are active in New York, Boston, Hanover, San Francisco, and Washington, D.C.

WHAT IS AN ASIAN PACIFIC AMERICAN (APA)?
APA is an abbreviation for "Asian Pacific American" which is a general term used to refer to Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders.

WHAT CONSTITUTES AS ASIAN OR ASIAN AMERICAN?
The College automatically includes anyone who self identifies as Asian upon matriculation as a DAPAAA member upon graduation. South Asians are also considered Asians.

ARE DAPAAA EVENTS OPEN TO NON-DAPAAA MEMBERS?
Events are open to DAPAAA members and their guests.

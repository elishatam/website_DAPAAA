Alumni Council Rep (3 Year Position)
Length of Term: 2011-2014 (the next Council term begins July 1, 2011)
  • Represents DAPAAA alumni at Alumni Council Meetings*
  • Participate in a minimum of 4 DAPAAA sponsored activities annually
  • Makes at least four formal reports to his/her constituency annually
  • Serves usually on one Council Committee
  • Serves as an official College representative & answers questions of alumni, bringing ideas, problems and comments of alumni to the Council
  • Thinks independently and deeply and works with the Trustees, faculty, students, and administration in a common effort to preserve the College's strengths and build her future.

Public Relations Chair
(2 Year Position, with option to extend up to 4 years)

Preferably in New York, Boston, or Hanover area.
The Public Relations Chair writes brief, witty emails, and plays the critical role of being an advocate, liaison, and spokesperson for DAPAAA and its constituents. The PR Chair is responsible for managing the internal and external communications needs of DAPAAA. Specific responsibilities include:
  • Create and distribute a biannual email summary of APA/ Dartmouth-related events and updates
  • Write short 120 word blurbs/updates for Dartmouth Alumni Magazine
  • Correspondence with Alumni Relations office on critical undergraduate and alumni events/issues
  • Maintain DAPAAA's facebook group & updates on website
  • Support the DAPAAA Co-Chairs and Regional Chairs in communicating APA / Dartmouth-related news and events relevant to the DAPAAA membership
  • Networking opportunities with leadership of other Ivy League Asian Alumni Orgs such as Princeton, Columbia, UPenn, etc.
  • Work closely with the board, Office for Alumni Diversity, & the Office of Pluralism and Leadership

Regional Coordinators
(2 Year Position, with option to extend up to 4 years)
Needed for Boston, Chicago, Bay Area, LA
(Up to 2 coordinators for each region)
  • Organize a minimum of 3 events per year in your city for the DAPAAA membership in your region. Examples of past events include: dimsum brunches, mentor lunches, happy hours, volunteer events, alumni interviewing.
  • Meet and work with Asian Pacific American alumni groups from other schools to organize joint events for our constituencies
  • Work with and participate in DAPAAA Board on issues relevant to DAPAAA membership
  • A 2 hr training session to learn the email/communications system is required. You will use this system to distribute communications to DAPAAA members in your region
